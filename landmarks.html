Sanctuary of Our Lady of the Rosary of Fátima

This Sanctuary is dedicated to "Our Lady of Fatima"
"Our Lady of Fatima" is a Catholic title for Mary, the mother of Jesus.
There are multiple Chapels such as the Sacred Lausperene and the Chapel of Apparitions.

The Sanctuary is located in Ourém, Portugal.

Belém Tower

The tower of Belém is officially the Tower of Saint Vincent.
It also served as a ceremonial gateway to Lisbon for explorers.
It has been a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1983.

The Tower of Belém is located in Lisbon, Portugal.

Park and National Palace of Pena

It was originally a Chapel dedicated to Our Lady of Pena
It is a National Monument of Portugal and one of the Seven Wonders of Portugal.
It is also a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Park and National Palace of Pena is located in Sintra, Portugal.
